What is DSCR?

Debt Service Coverage Ratio = Annual Rental Income ÷ Annual Mortgage Payment (PITIA).

Example: Property rents for $3,000/mo ($36K/yr). Mortgage payment is $2,000/mo ($24K/yr). DSCR = $36K ÷ $24K = 1.50.

Quick reference:

  • 1.00 — Property breaks even
  • 1.15 — 15% positive cash flow (qualifies most programs)
  • 1.25+ — Strong cash flow (best pricing)
